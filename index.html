About Us

This year, HackCU will host Local Hack Day on December 2nd, 2017 at the University of Colorado, Boulder! Local Hack Day is a worldwide designed to bring students of technology together for just 12 hours to build something amazing.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where motivated people come together and use technology and creativity to build something amazing.

Who can attend?

Any current student! The hackathon is open to designers, writers, computer scientists or anyone interested in hackathons.

What should I bring?

Laptop, chargers, comfortable clothes, and a water bottle. We'll provide you everything else you need.

Do you offer travel reimbursement?

No, everyone attending Local Hack Day should be local. If you're out of state, check out HackCU IV where we offer reimbursement!

Will there be food?

Yes, lots! We will provide three main meals as well as snacks, drinks, and caffeine.

What about teams?

You can have a maximum of 4 members on your team. There will time before the hacking begins where we will help you to find a team.

How can I help?

We always need volunteers and mentors! Email us for more info.

Need to contact us?

Shoot us a message on Facebook or Twitter, or Email us!
